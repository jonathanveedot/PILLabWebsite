About Us

 

The Pharmacy Innovation Launch Lab (PILLab) is a think tank organization that aims to solve the pharmacy problems of tomorrow, today. In essence, we are (re)imagining pharmacy.

Before officially organizing and advancing our mission, we were a group of faculty and students brought together by a course offered by the Univeristy of Pittsburgh School of Pharmacy, "Contemporary Issues in Pharmacy". This collection of passionate thinkers came together to discuss potential solutions to issues and problems in healthcare. These discussions provided a setting for ideas to develop into actions, and this group soon evolved into an organization where the practical application of information is used to address critical issues in healthcare.

The School of Pharmacy's PILLab is now an association of faculty and students that collaborate with members of other departments and schools, hospitals, long-term care pharmacies, retail chains, and patients. At the PILLab we firmly believe that collaboration inspires innovation.

 

 

Mission Statement

The PILLab aims to (Re)Imagine advancements in healthcare by providing information solutions that activate consumers and connect providers. We rapidly prototype solutions to common problems in healthcare, and advance the quality of our systems through an iterative evaluation and design that includes all stakeholders. Our motto is "fail quick, fail small, win big".
