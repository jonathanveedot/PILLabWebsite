WHO WE ARE

The Pharmacy Innovation Launch Lab (PILLab) at the Univeristy of Pittsburgh is a team of students, faculty, champions, and creative thinkers who aim to reimagine the future of pharmacy.

WHAT WE DO

We engage in a creative curriculum that shapes students into tomorrow's innovators of pharmacy. We conduct research to learn how to change today's practice while partnering with leaders in healthcare to make the future a reality.





Learning Tech Lab Collabs

We often complement our domain expertise with the technical expertise of the Learning Technologies Lab at the School of Computing and Information as we design innovative solutions for today's problems in pharmacy.
