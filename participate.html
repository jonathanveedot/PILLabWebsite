Partners

What we can do for you

To help reimagine the future, we partner with other academic institutions, companies, and individuals. Our partnerships support the analysis and development of information solutions in pharmacy, and include services such as the validation of concept ideas, planning the iterative development of collaborative solutions, and access to current project outputs.

If you’re interested in learning how you can partner with our team of faculty, students, and other partners, don’t hesitate to contact us*.

 

Students

Let us support your creativity to innovate pharmacy

Do you have an idea, but don't quite know how to bring it to life? Are you looking to advance your knowledge and skills by working toward solving real-world problems? The PILLab is always excited to work with students interested in learning more about innovating pharmacy operations and practice.

Please contact us* more information about participating in projects, competitions, and courses.

Projects

Participate in one of our many ongoing projects*, or come with an idea of your own and let’s see what we can do together. Our projects require various kinds of knowledge and skill, and you will be sure to add breadth to your repertoire as your learn about the research and development of information solutions in pharmacy.

Competitions

There are many competitions in which students can earn funding for project ideas. The PILLab encourages students to participate in these events, and we are happy to help facilitate team creation, offer mentorship, or provide feedback on pitches and presentations. PInCh*, the Randall Family Big Idea Competition*, and the Wells Competition* are a few examples of some of the excellent events in which we have supported students.

Courses

You can make innovation a part of your education by enrolling in Pharmacy Innovation 1 or Pharmacy Innovation 2, offered in the Fall and Spring semesters, respectively. Additionally, we supervise student projects during a Special Topics elective course or an Advanced Pharmacy Practice Experience (APPE) rotation.
